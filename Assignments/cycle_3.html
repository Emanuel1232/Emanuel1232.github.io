Canvas

When creating my vinyl design I had been tasked with creating animage that consisted of two different colors. For my design I chose to make an orange and yellow die.

Using adobe illustrator's selection tool I manipulated three squares into rhombuses and seven circles into ovals. Then I filled all the shapes with black and layered my design. The first layer held the die with the ovals cut out and the second with the ovals filled in with both layers essentially creating a black cube.

After having the finish product on one sheet of transfer paper I was free to use my freshly printed design as a sticker.
