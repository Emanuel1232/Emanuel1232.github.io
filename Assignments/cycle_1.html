With being assigned the task of creating a puzzle with a 2D image, I chose to make my image a crocodile doing a tailgrab on a skateboard. Using adobe illustrator to first, raserize the image and then to set the borders to .01mm for a vector cut on the laser.

Afterwards, I locked the layer the image then used the "Elipse", "Unite" and "Subtract" tools in adobe illustrator and proceeded to design the puzzle pieces/layout on an overlapping layer with the so the image of Tony Croc was still visible. When all pieces were completed I saved the file as a ".ai" file.

Finally, after sending the rasterized image to the laser cutter I turned on all air vents to the laser cutter as well as air assist to reeduce the chance of my project catching fire. After adjusting the origin and ofcus of the laser, I cut my puzzle under the supervision of my professor.
