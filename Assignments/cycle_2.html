Being tasked to design a container for a specific object, I chose to do an uno card box. This was my conatiner of choice because growing up, the box that uno cards were packaged in were inevitably replaced by a rubber band. Using Fusion 360 I designede a 3D model of what the box would look like.

Making the inside of the box wide enough to hold an uno card and large enough to house a deck, I then decided to work on the lid. I designed the lid so that it fits snugly on top of the box in a joint like fashion while also adding two slots on either side of the lid allowing me to remove it without turning the box upside down.

After taking roughly four and a half hours to print in the prusaslicer, I cleaned uo the dges of the box and rubbed it down with some alcohol so it wouldn't be sticky.
